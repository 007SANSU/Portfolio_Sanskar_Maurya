About

From behaviour, to hypothesis, to shipped fix.

I'm a Product Manager who lives close to the data. My day moves from pulling and segmenting users in SQL, to mapping journeys and drop-offs in GA4 and Mixpanel, to KPI dashboards a stakeholder can act on in seconds — then into the PRD that turns the insight into something engineering can build.

I'm drawn to high-consideration e-commerce and marketplace funnels, where a single number can reshape the customer journey.
